Eligibility Criteria to Apply

Here are the key eligibility criteria for the Odisha Subhadra Yojana:

  • State Residency:

    The applicant must be a permanent resident of Odisha.Only female applicants are eligible, Every married and unmarried woman of the family is eligible to apply.

  • Anganwadi Workers & Asha Workers:

    Special benefits are extended to Anganwadi workers under this scheme. (If your Husband is a government employee then you are not eligible.) Special benefits are extended to Asha Didi workers under this scheme. (If your Husband is a government employee then you are not eligible.)

  • Age Range,Income &Employment Status:

    The applicant should be between 21 and 60 years old of 01.07.2024. Annual family income should be Rs 2.5 lakh or less. The applicant should not be employed in any government sector.

Benefits of Odisha Subhadra Yojana

Each eligible beneficiary woman will receive a total of INR.50,000 in 5 years. Rs 10,000 per year, to be paid in two installments. The first installment will be paid on Raksha Bandhan, and the second on International Women’s Day (March 8). The funds will be deposited directly into the beneficiaries’ Aadhaar-linked bank accounts, and they will also receive a Subhadra Debit Card. Budget Rs. 55,825 crore allocated.
